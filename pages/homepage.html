Tropical Paradise.

Welcome to Mauritius – your gateway to paradise! Picture this: sun-kissed beaches, vibrant coral reefs and exuberant green landscapes that make you want to explore every corner. Mauritius is not just about magnificent beaches and clear blue waters – though we have plenty of those! It’s a vibrant tapestry of cultures, flavours and experiences just waiting for you to dive in.


Whether you're here to relax on the beach, explore the wild, or immerse yourself in our vibrant culture, this island promises indelible memories at every turn. Your paradise adventure begins now

Did you know?

It’s a small island but with so much to see!
Mauritius proudly hosted the esteemed Jeux des Îles 2019.
Mauritius is the happiest country in Africa.
Mauritius is surrounded by a ring of dormant volcanoes, streams, waterfalls and rivers in all four directions.
Life expectancy is a reasonably high at 75 years in Mauritius.
Mauritius embodies a rich tapestry of multicultural diversity.
Every Mauritian knows at least 3 languages: Creole, English and French
Mauritius' peaceful atmosphere allows it to exist without needing to raise a standing army.
