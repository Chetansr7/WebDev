5. Black River Gorges National park

Whether you're into serious hiking or just want to enjoy a peaceful nature walk, Black River Gorges has something for everyone. It's a fantastic spot to connect with nature and escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. It's the island's largest national park, covering a vast area of lush forest, dramatic gorges, and stunning waterfalls. The park is a haven for diverse wildlife, including endemic birds like the Mauritius kestrel and the echo parakeet. The hiking trails offer breathtaking views and lead you through dense forests and past scenic lookout points.
