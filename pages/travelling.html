Travelling to Mauritius

How to get to Mauritius?

Mauritius is an island in the Indian Ocean, so you will need to catch a flight to Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport in Mauritius or arrive by cruise ship, which will dock in our capital city of Port Louis. Mauritius is really well connected, with direct flights from Europe, South Africa, the UAE, Malaysia, India and Australia. You can also find out more about our national airline, Air Mauritius.

A quick guide to direct flights to Mauritius

-Cruise to Mauritius-

You can visit Mauritius during a cruise onboard big-named liners, including Cunard’s iconic Queen Mary and Queen Elizabeth ships. Mauritius cruises are gaining in popularity and Port Louis harbour in the northwest of the island welcomes luxury expedition cruise liners sailing from countries including Italy, France, Monaco, the UK, Germany and South Africa. Cruise itineraries usually sail the Vanilla Islands circuit, which includes Mauritius, Seychelles, Reunion Island and Madagascar.

New cruise terminal in Mauritius

To offer a quality service to guests and ships berthing in Mauritius, a new cruise terminal has been built in Port Louis and is operational since December 2023. The new Mauritius Port terminal is one of the best in the Indian Ocean, with the capacity to accommodate 4000 passengers each day.
