Accommodation in Mauritius

In Mauritius, tourists have a wide range of accommodation options to suit various preferences and budgets. From luxurious beach resorts and boutique hotels to cozy guesthouses and self-catering apartments, the island offers something for everyone. Many accommodations boast stunning ocean views, proximity to tourist attractions, and amenities such as spas, pools, and water sports facilities. Whether you're looking for a relaxing beachfront stay or a cultural immersion in the island's inland regions, Mauritius provides diverse choices to enhance your holiday experience.

Take a look at the accommodation types in Mauritius and start planning!

Hotels

Mauritius offers a captivating selection of hotels, each promising a unique blend of comfort and luxury. Whether you're drawn to beachfront resorts with panoramic ocean views or boutique hotels nestled in lush gardens, the island caters to every taste and preference. Discover a haven where impeccable service meets tranquil surroundings, providing the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable getaway in Mauritius.

Villas

Escape to the enchanting island of Mauritius and stay in one of its luxurious villas, designed to provide an unforgettable holiday experience. These stunning accommodations offer the perfect blend of elegance and tranquility, with each villa featuring spacious living areas, lush gardens, and private pools. Wake up to the sound of the ocean, enjoy al fresco dining with panoramic views, and immerse yourself in the island's rich culture and natural beauty. With dedicated concierge services, you can easily explore Mauritius' attractions, from its vibrant markets to its pristine beaches and scenic nature trails. Whether you seek adventure or relaxation, villas in Mauritius offer an idyllic retreat that caters to all your desires.

AirBnB

Experience the vibrant culture and natural beauty of Mauritius with a stay at one of the many unique Airbnb accommodations available on the island. From charming beachfront bungalows to modern city apartments, Airbnb offers a diverse range of options to suit every traveler's needs and budget. Enjoy the warmth of Mauritian hospitality as you stay in locally-owned properties, providing an authentic and personalized touch to your holiday. Whether you're looking for a cozy retreat in the mountains, a stylish home in the heart of the city, or a serene cottage by the sea, Airbnb hosts in Mauritius go the extra mile to ensure a memorable stay. Embrace the island life with the flexibility and comfort of Airbnb, making your Mauritian adventure truly one-of-a-kind.

Lodge

-Get up close to nature-

Immerse yourself in the natural beauty and tranquility of Mauritius by staying at one of the island's charming lodges. Perfectly nestled in lush landscapes, these lodges offer an intimate and rustic retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Wake up to the melodies of tropical birds, explore nearby hiking trails, or simply relax on your private veranda with stunning views of verdant forests and crystal-clear lagoons. With personalized service and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, lodges in Mauritius provide a cozy and authentic experience, blending comfort with the island’s rich biodiversity. Whether you're looking for a secluded hideaway or a base for exploring Mauritius' natural wonders, these lodges offer a unique and unforgettable stay.

Homestay

Homestays in Mauritius offer a unique and immersive experience for tourists seeking to connect deeply with local culture and hospitality. Staying with Mauritian families provides a firsthand glimpse into daily life, traditions, and authentic Mauritian cuisine. Hosts often warmly welcome guests into their homes, sharing stories and local insights that guide visitors beyond typical tourist experiences. Whether nestled in coastal villages, lush mountainsides, or bustling towns, homestays offer a cozy retreat where guests can relax in a familial atmosphere after exploring Mauritius' renowned beaches, nature reserves, and cultural sites. It's an excellent choice for travelers seeking a more personalized and enriching vacation experience in this beautiful island paradise.
